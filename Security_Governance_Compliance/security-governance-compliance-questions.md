# Security, Governance & Compliance — Migration Questions

| Category | Role | Question |
|---|---|---|
| Security | Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) | What perimeter and cloud security controls will protect customer data during the migration? |
| Security | Head of IT Security | How will encryption be applied for data at rest and in transit throughout the migration process? |
| Security | IT Security Manager | What identity and access management (IAM) changes are required for migrated systems and how will least-privilege be enforced? |
| Security | Application Security Lead | How will application-level vulnerabilities introduced by migration be identified, tracked, and remediated? |
| Security | Network Security Lead | What network segmentation, firewall and VPN controls will isolate migrated workloads from sensitive systems? |
| Security | Incident Response Lead | What is the incident detection and response plan if a security breach occurs during or after migration? |
| Governance | Chief Risk Officer (CRO) | What risk acceptance criteria apply to this migration and who has authority to sign off on residual risk? |
| Governance | Chief Information Officer (CIO) | Who is the accountable owner for migrated services and what SLAs and KPIs will they report against? |
| Governance | Head of Architecture | How will the architecture review board evaluate and approve designs for migrated components? |
| Governance | Vendor Management Lead | How will third-party and supplier governance be enforced for cloud or service providers used in the migration? |
| Governance | Business Unit Head | What executive reporting and governance cadence will track migration progress, issues and risks? |
| Governance | Change Manager | What change control, cutover windows and rollback criteria govern migration activities? |
| Compliance | Head of Compliance | Which Indian and sectoral regulatory frameworks (e.g., Reserve Bank of India guidelines, Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act or applicable Indian data protection law, Information Technology Act, SEBI rules as applicable) must the migration satisfy and how will compliance be demonstrated to Indian regulators? |
| Compliance | Data Protection Officer (DPO) | How will personal data inventories, legal bases for processing, consent records, and data mapping be updated and documented to meet Indian DPDP/IT Act requirements for migrated data? |
| Compliance | Privacy Officer | What data retention, minimization, and deletion policies apply post-migration under Indian law (including any RBI retention guidance), and how will these policies be enforced and evidenced? |
| Compliance | Anti‑Money Laundering (AML) Officer | How will transaction monitoring, KYC and AML controls be preserved, validated, and remain compliant with Indian AML/KYC regulations (PMLA, RBI and FIU-IND guidance) during the migration? |
| Compliance | Internal Audit Lead | What audit artifacts, immutable trails and logs will be produced and retained to demonstrate compliance for Indian regulator inspections and internal/external audits? |
| Compliance | Legal Counsel | How will contractual obligations, jurisdictional constraints and India-specific cross‑border data transfer rules be identified and addressed for migrated data, including cloud-provider contracts and Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards? |
